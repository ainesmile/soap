Baby food packet meals

At Countdown we know that life with littlies is busy. With all our baby products we aim to give you convenient choices which help keep babies and toddlers healthy and happy, so our baby food range includes some readymade baby cereal pouches made by Farex.

Once you are supplementing baby formula or breastmilk with solids, these can be really useful standbys for mornings when you need quick breakfast options. And they're great to throw in baby bags for days out or when travelling. Containing no added colours, preservatives, sugar or flavours, and iron enriched, they are made from the very best ingredients and taste great.
